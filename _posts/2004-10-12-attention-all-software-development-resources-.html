--- layout: post title: Attention all software development "resources"! published: 1 category: - Programming ---

Every software developer who's tired of being called a "resource," raise your hand.

I can scarcely think of a more dehumanizing way to label someone that you'd like to think was hired because of their intelligence, creativity, and motivation in solving software problems, rather than for their resemblance to a lump of coal, gallon of gasoline, or bag of Fritos (thanks, David Nunn, for the Frito analogy).
