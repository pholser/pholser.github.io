--- layout: post title: JOpt Simple 3.0-beta1 released published: 1 category: - Programming ---

JOpt Simple is my Java library for parsing command-line options.  Release 3.0 will abandon support for pre-Java-5 JDKs, in favor of the new more typesafe ways of retrieving arguments of command line options. I will continue to support the 2.x line of development as needed. Give JOpt Simple's new look a test drive, and let me know what can be improved.
