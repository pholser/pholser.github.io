--- layout: post title: "The Underground History of American Education" published: 1 category: - Books ---

The great John Taylor Gatto, former New York State and City Teacher of the Year, has produced a scathing indictment of forced public schooling, bringing to light the many players behind the scenes and framing some of the boredom, fear, and general anxiety I endured during my school days. And I was supposedly one of the "gifted and talented" students...

Check out "The Underground History of American Education", quickly, before your children's sparks are extinguished.
